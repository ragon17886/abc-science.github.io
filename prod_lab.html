Science Lab Chemistry Set - 238+ Experiments

This comprehensive science kit for children offers over 238 safe experiments in chemistry and physics. The set includes measuring utensils, test tubes, pipettes, a funnel, a stand, safety goggles, a set of reagents, and a detailed illustrated instruction manual. Children can mix solutions, observe capillary action, density, acid and base reactions, study polymers and colors. An excellent gift for young researchers and a visual aid for STEM lessons.

Features:

  • 238+ experiments - from simple color reactions to mini-projects lasting 15-30 minutes
  • 80+ items and consumables included in the set
  • Safe for learning - simple reagents, convenient measuring containers, safety goggles
  • Step-by-step guide book with pictures and explanations of scientific principles
  • Develops curiosity, logic, accuracy, and laboratory skills
  • For children from 5 years (with adult support)

What's Included:

  • Safety goggles
  • Test tubes with lids and stand
  • Measuring cups and flasks (with scale)
  • Pipettes/droppers, spoons, stirring sticks
  • Funnel, dispenser bottle
  • Packages with reagents and dyes (e.g., pigments, acid/baking soda, polymer granules, etc.)
  • Illustrated instructions and experiment cards

Example Experiments:

  • Rainbow in a glass (layers of different density)
  • "Lava lamp" in a bottle
  • Capillarity in leaves/cabbage and color change
  • Foam volcano (acid + baking soda)
  • Rainbow milk with detergent
  • Hydrogel balls and polymers
  • Candle and water trick, crystallization, etc.
