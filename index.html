Approved Educational Materials for Scholarship Programs

ABC-Science LLC provides high-quality, program-approved educational kits and materials for families participating in state scholarship programs like ESA, EFA, GATOR, and WESA. Our products are designed to meet the criteria for instructional materials and hands-on learning resources.

We specialize in STEM education materials that help students explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through engaging, practical experiments and activities that align with educational standards and program requirements.

Program Approved Materials

All our educational materials are carefully selected to meet the criteria for various state scholarship programs. We ensure our products qualify as instructional materials, curriculum supplements, and hands-on learning resources that support educational goals and learning objectives.

Our kits are designed to help students explore core knowledge domains including science, mathematics, and technology through engaging, practical experiments that align with educational standards.

Supporting Special Education Needs

We specialize in providing educational resources that support students with special education needs. Our materials are excellent for individualized education plans and tailored learning approaches in various educational settings including homeschool, microschools, and home-based education.

Many of our kits support multi-sensory learning approaches and can be adapted for different learning styles and abilities.

Compliance & Documentation

We understand the importance of compliance with program requirements. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that all products meet the criteria for allowable expenses and qualifying educational materials under various state programs.

We provide necessary documentation and support to help families successfully purchase our materials using their scholarship accounts, education freedom accounts, or microgrant funds.
