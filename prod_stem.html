This multifunctional wooden STEM kit allows you to build 10 fascinating models that introduce children to the fundamentals of physics, mechanics, engineering, and technology. All parts of the set are made of eco-friendly wood, making the assembly process not only educational but also safe.

What can be built from the kit:

  • Hydraulic Excavator - A model with two syringes demonstrating the principles of hydraulics and construction equipment operation.
  • Elevator Lift - A mechanical elevator model with electric drive or manual control.
  • Lifting Table (Hydraulic Lift) - Demonstrates the operation of lifting platforms using hydraulics.
  • Suspension Bridge - An engineering structure model illustrating the laws of bridge strength and stability.
  • Catapult - A classic mechanical catapult for launching small objects.
  • Ballista - An ancient projectile throwing machine.
  • Rubber Band Car - A model car powered by a twisted rubber band.
  • Electric Car - A car powered by an electric motor, controlled via remote.
  • Drawing Pendulum (Spirograph) - A device that creates patterns through rotation and pendulum movements.
  • Snake Lift (Serpentine Elevator) - A mechanical model demonstrating movement along a complex trajectory.

Benefits of the kit:

  • Develops logical thinking, motor skills, engineering, and spatial imagination.
  • Allows studying the basics of physics, mechanics, hydraulics, electrical engineering, and architecture.
  • Excellent for joint activities of parents and children, as well as for school projects.
